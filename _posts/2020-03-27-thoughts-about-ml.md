Over the recent few years, we've witnessed the explosion of Machine Learning, almost everyone has heard about it. we've seen so many titles on the famous magazines and newspapers talking about Machine Learning, so many startups are forming everyday and big companies are rebuilding themselves about machine learning, there is a strong belief that ML is the next Big Thing.

Machine learning is all about exploiting patterns in data. it is about finding patterns or structures in data that are implicit, by inference, rather than explicit. this kind of patterns previously could be found by only humans not computers, they address a specific class of questions that are easy for humans and hard for computers, in other words, Hard for people to describe to the computers.

There is still a great debate about the what machine learning means and what it will mean for large tech companies or economy or for all rest of us, there is still no clear way to think structurly about the new things ML can enable, or what is the important problems it might solve.

there are alot unhelpul thoughts that unfortunately very popular and lots of people are convinced with it like data is the new oil, big tech companies, here I mean specifically FAANAGM companies, and China have all the data, another really misconcept one that AI will take over all the jobs and many people will lose their jobs. the most common misconeption that comes when talking about machine learning that it is in someway a single tool that leads to the General Artificial intelligence. These thoughts are oftern arises because of movies from 20's that presented robots as metal humans moving around in the factories with hummers in their hands, robots that moving in houses assisting in housework, driving and controlling spaceships and too many applications that figures the robotics and artificial intelligence as a human like creature that can resonate, think and act generally tackling too many different tasks in different environments. Actually this thought is far away from the reality.

a more proper way to think about Machine learning is seeing it as a kind of new wave of automation and an enabling way for new techonology layers. it's a good way to think about ML as a step change in what we can do with our computers today, and that it will be part of many different products. Eventually, everything will have ML incorporated inside and no one will ever notice or care!.
